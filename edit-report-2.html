Please select the type of dangerous goods event this notification relates to

Accident

Accidents relate to an event associated with and related to the transport of dangerous goods by air which results in fatal or serious injury to a person or major property or environmental damage.

Incident

Incidents relate to an event, other than a dangerous goods accident, associated with and related to the transport of dangerous goods by air, not necessarily occurring on board an aircraft, which results in injury to a person, property or environmental damage, fire, breakage, spillage, leakage of fluid or radiation or other evidence that the integrity of the packaging has not been maintained. Any occurrence relating to the transport of dangerous goods which seriously jeopardizes the aircraft or its occupants is also deemed to be a dangerous goods incident.

Undeclared

Undeclared relate to an event where dangerous goods which should have been declared and are subsequently discovered in cargo or mail.

Misdeclared

Misdeclared relates to an event where dangerous goods that have been incorrectly classified or declared incorrectly and are subsequently discovered in cargo or mail.

Occurance

Occurance relates to dangerous goods that are discovered to have been carried when not loaded, segregated, separated or secured in accordance with the ICAO Technical Instructions; or that are discovered to have been carried without information having been provided to the pilot-in-command accordance with the ICAO Technical Instructions.
